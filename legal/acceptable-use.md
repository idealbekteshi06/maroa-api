# Acceptable Use Policy

**Effective date:** <<EFFECTIVE_DATE>>

This Acceptable Use Policy is part of the Terms of Service. Violations
may result in suspension or termination without refund.

## You may NOT use Maroa to:

### Content + speech

- Generate or publish content that is **illegal** in the recipient's
  jurisdiction (defamation, fraud, harassment, child exploitation,
  hate speech, threats)
- Create **deceptive** content that misrepresents your products, identity,
  or affiliations
- Run **clickbait** or **engagement-bait** schemes that violate platform terms
- Generate **medical**, **legal**, or **financial advice** posing as a
  qualified professional
- Promote **adult content**, **gambling**, **weapons**, **drugs**, or
  **other restricted categories** without complying with all applicable
  platform + jurisdictional rules

### Spam + abuse

- Send unsolicited messages at scale (email, DM, SMS, WhatsApp)
- Buy or scrape email lists / phone numbers
- Operate fake review schemes (Maroa's VOC tool reads real reviews; never
  use it to generate fake ones)
- Run **astroturfing** campaigns that misrepresent organic engagement

### Platform abuse

- Violate the **Terms of Service** of any connected platform (Meta,
  Google, LinkedIn, TikTok, etc.) — including but not limited to their
  Community Standards, Ad Policies, and Developer Policies
- Use Meta / Google ad credentials in ways that violate their
  prohibited content lists
- Resell account access to other parties

### Technical abuse

- **Reverse-engineer**, decompile, or scrape Maroa
- **Probe** for security vulnerabilities outside an authorized
  security-research engagement (email **security@maroa.ai** first)
- **Bypass rate limits**, cost caps, or plan limits via multiple
  accounts, automation, or other means
- Use Maroa for **denial-of-service** activities against third parties
- Use Maroa to generate, run, or distribute **malware** or **phishing**
  content

### Identity + impersonation

- Use **fake identities** or stolen credentials
- Impersonate Maroa staff, customers, public figures, or organizations
  you don't represent
- Create accounts on behalf of others without authorization

### Intellectual property

- Generate content that **infringes** copyright, trademark, or other
  IP rights of third parties
- Use Maroa to **deepfake** real people (likeness, voice, video)
  without consent + necessary disclosures
- Train competing AI models on Maroa output (output is yours, but
  scraping our prompts / system messages is not allowed)

## High-risk categories — additional rules

For political ads, financial services, health products, alcohol, and
similar regulated categories, you must:

- Comply with all platform-specific rules (Meta political ad
  authorization, Google financial-services certification, etc.)
- Maintain accurate **identity disclosures** as required by law
- Provide Maroa with proof of certification on request

## Security research

We welcome responsible security research:

- Email **security@maroa.ai** before testing
- Stay within your own account / dedicated test account
- No DOS, no social engineering of staff, no data exfiltration beyond
  what's needed to demonstrate
- We commit to acknowledging reports within 5 business days

## Enforcement

We may, at our discretion:

- **Warn** you about a potential violation
- **Suspend** publishing to specific platforms while we investigate
- **Suspend** your account pending resolution
- **Terminate** your account for severe or repeated violations
- **Report** to law enforcement when required or warranted

Termination for AUP violation does **not** trigger refunds beyond the
mandatory 7-day cooling-off period.

## Reporting

If you believe a Maroa customer is violating this policy:

- **Email**: abuse@maroa.ai
- We respond within 2 business days

## Contact

- **Security research**: security@maroa.ai
