# Maroa vs Claude for Small Business (2026)

Anthropic's **Claude for Small Business** bundles connectors (HubSpot, Canva, QuickBooks) and 15 generic workflows inside Claude Cowork. It targets the same SMB owner Maroa serves.

## Where Maroa wins (keep investing here)

1. **Paid media execution** — Meta + Google campaign create/optimize/pause with plan gates and anti-thrashing (`ad-optimizer`, `meta-campaigns`).
2. **Always-on ops** — Inngest crons (content, crisis, scorecard, competitor briefs) — not chat-only.
3. **Quality gate** — Nothing ships without slop/specificity/brand-voice checks.
4. **Cost discipline** — `llmGateway`, web search caps, batch 50% pricing, cache diagnostics.
5. **Marketing-specific data model** — `analytics_snapshots`, `competitor_briefs`, `ad_performance_logs`, not a generic CRM sidebar.

## Where Anthropic wins (don't rebuild; integrate or ignore)

- Native **QuickBooks / payroll** — out of scope unless Agency ERP add-on.
- **Canva in-chat editing** — Maroa generates via Higgsfield + approval flow instead.
- **HubSpot in Claude chat** — optional future: HubSpot MCP for Agency tier only.

## Product narrative (for site + sales)

> "Claude for Small Business helps you chat with your tools. **Maroa runs your marketing** — posts, ads, competitor intel, and weekly scorecards — with guardrails and real platform APIs."

## 90-day differentiation checklist

- [ ] Ship Threads placements to 10% of Growth campaigns (measure CPA).
- [ ] Agency monthly batch content calendar (extended output).
- [ ] Code-execution monthly PDF in scorecard email.
- [ ] Public "Maroa MCP" for power users (distribution).
